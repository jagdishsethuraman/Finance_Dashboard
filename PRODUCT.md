# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Self-hosting tech-savvy developer and personal finance enthusiast tracking family net worth, budgets, and asset allocation locally.

## Product Purpose
Compile all family finance data in one secure place with zero cloud dependencies, relying on local parsing and databases.

## Brand Personality
Minimalist, Premium, Technical

## Anti-references
Generic neon-gradient SaaS, over-designed mobile banking, complex enterprise accounting software.

## Design Principles
1. Privacy First: All computations, data storage (SQLite), and parsing (local Ollama) run locally. No third-party analytics or tracker leaks.
2. Classic Technical Aesthetic: Structured geometric layouts, wide spacing, Satoshi typography, and monospaced Geist Mono for numbers.
3. High Information Density with Restraint: Structured bento grids and charts that emphasize clarity over decorative styling.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- Strict contrast compliance (>= 4.5:1) for body text and interactive states against dark background.
- Respect prefers-reduced-motion media query for all transitions.
